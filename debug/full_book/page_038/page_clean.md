14.Bald eagles are known to fly as high as 4500 - 6000 meters above the ground level. Mount Everest is about 8850 meters high. Aeroplanes can fly as high as 10,000 - 12,800 meters.

How many times bigger are these heights compared to Somu's building?

(i) Height of Somu's building = 40 meters

(a) Bald eagle's flight height (lower estimate) = 4500 meters
   4500 ÷ 40 = 112.5
   Bald eagles can fly approximately 112.5 times higher than Somu's building.

(b) Bald eagle's flight height (upper estimate) = 6000 meters
   6000 ÷ 40 = 150
   Bald eagles can fly approximately 150 times higher than Somu's building.

(ii) Height of Mount Everest = 8850 meters
   8850 ÷ 40 = 221.25
   Mount Everest is approximately 221 times taller than Somu's building.

(iii) Aeroplane's flight height (lower estimate) = 10,000 meters
   10,000 ÷ 40 = 250
   Aeroplanes can fly approximately 250 times as high as Somu's building.

(iv) Aeroplane's flight height (upper estimate) = 12,800 meters
   12,800 ÷ 40 = 320
   Aeroplanes can fly approximately 320 times as high as Somu's building.
