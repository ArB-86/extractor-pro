(a) One lakh twenty three thousand four hundred and fifty-six
(b) Four lakh seventy thousand seven hundred and four
(c) Fifty lakhs five thousand and fifty

Note to the Teacher: Encourage students to make connections between these facts.
For example, can the whole population of Chintamani fit in a stadium? How can we imagine a line of 38 km, having fifty thousand people, sitting next to each other in the stadium?
