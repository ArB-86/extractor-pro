# Fascinating Facts about Large Numbers

Some interesting facts about large numbers are hidden below. Calculate the product or quotient to uncover the facts. Once you find the product or quotient, read the number in both Indian and American naming or systems. Share thoughts and questions about the fact with your class after discovering each number.

This is the number of kirtanas composed by Purandaradasa according to legends.
Purandaradasa was a composer and singer in the 15th century. His kirtanas spanned social reform, bhakti, and spirituality. He systematised methods for teaching Carnatic music which is followed to this day.
